DNP3 PCAPS

NOTE: There are no malicious binary payloads in ANY of these files.  There may be anomaly injected using fuzzing techniques.


*2M-Packets-DNP3.pcap
2M overall packets w/multiple DNP3 methods.  Clean data with no injects

*DNP3_20000.pcap
DEV/Test Environments Standard 20000 port numbers.  This is from a development environment but is "clean" data, meaning true to the spec.

*DNP3non-standard.pcap
DNP3 Fuzzing PCAPS used to test specific DNP3 fuzzing attacks

*Pcap-dnp3-only.pcap
Just lots of redundant DNP3 data, limited hosts
